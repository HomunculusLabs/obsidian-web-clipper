A delicious test recipe for testing purposes.

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 30 mins
Total: 45 mins
Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup milk

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Add wet ingredients and stir until combined.
  4. Pour into prepared pan and bake for 30 minutes.
  5. Let cool before serving.
